INITIATION 1100M


CHIEF FIRE THUNDER (App M Airy), which was difficult to be loaded and caused some delay, will be required to pass one starting stall test prior to being entered to race again in terms of MRA Rule 78(2).

CHYNA JADE (I Saifudin) was slow to begin and was crowded shortly after between CHIEF FIRE THUNDER and DRAGON STRONG (M Zaki).

BIGNMIGHTY (App M Ewe), which began awkwardly and shifted inwards abruptly, lost further ground when steadied away from the heels of CHYNA JADE.

Approaching the 700m, INCREDIBLE KING (App TR Barnabas) steadied after racing up onto the heels of CAPTAIN SEQUALO (J Ford).

Near the 700m, JACK OF DIAMONDS (M Cahill) and DRAGON STRONG were both momentarily steadied whilst racing tight to the inside of AUSSIE ONE (N Callow).

Passing the 600m, JACK OF DIAMONDS checked after it hung outwards across the heels of DRAGON STRONG. TRIGGER SYNERGY (P Knuckey), which was following, was hampered in consequence.

Approaching the 500m, INCREDIBLE KING, which was overracing, blundered when awkwardly placed onto the heels of CAPTAIN SEQUALO.

Over the concluding stages, SHANGHAI KID (J Powell) shifted outwards under pressure carrying JACK OF DIAMONDS wider on the track.

AUSSIE ONE, which raced greenly in the straight, shifted outwards under pressure over the concluding stages.

DRAGON STRONG laid inwards under pressure in the straight.

Near the 300m, CHIEF FIRE THUNDER momentarily steadied when crowded to the inside of SKY SPRINTER (KK Ng). Jockey KK Ng was subsequently shown the film of the incident and warned to exercise greater care when shifting ground in future.


The Farrier reported that CHIEF FIRE THUNDER returned having cast its off-hind plate.

Jockey Saifudin, when questioned regarding the performance of CHYNA JADE, stated that after being slow to begin, he was then crowded slightly. He added that despite his urging, his mount refused to stretch out in the early stages but after passing the 700m, his mount then continued to make up steady ground. His explanation was noted. A subsequent veterinary examination was requested on CHYNA JADE which revealed no apparent abnormality. CHYNA JADE will be required to pass a 1000m gallop trial from the barrier and a veterinary examination prior to being entered to race again.

RACE 4 [TF4]
CLASS 5 1000M


LUCKY DOMINO (App M Ewe) stood flat-footed and lost ground. In view of its previous record and as LUCKY DOMINO is a repeat offender, it will be barred from racing or entering for a test trial for a period of fourteen days, after which it must pass two consecutive starting stall tests.

MAJESTIC GLORY (Alan Ng) began awkwardly.

Shortly after the start, ONE AND ONLY (J Powell) and GERONIMO (App M Airy) were both severely crowded between BIG MOMENT (N Callow), which shifted outwards and ARKIN (M Cahill), which shifted inwards.

Near the 800m, GOTSPEED (R Woodworth) checked when racing to the inside of MAJESTIC GLORY, which despite the best efforts of its rider laid inwards away from PRIOR KING (I Saifudin).

WORD OF HONOUR (App JL Li) was obliged to race wide throughout without cover and laid inwards in the straight.

Jockey Cahill, when questioned regarding the poor performance of ARKIN, stated that he was instructed to lead if possible but after his mount began awkwardly and shifted inwards abruptly, he was unable to obtain a leading position and settled off-pace. He added that his mount laid inwards badly in the backstraight and again approaching the hometurn and disappointed with its failure to run on in the straight. His explanation was noted. ARKIN was impounded for routine veterinary and analytical examination. The Veterinary Surgeon reported that ARKIN bled. In terms of MRA Rule 92(3), it will not be permitted to canter or gallop on the main training tracks of any racecourse for a period of two months, and may not start in any race for a period of three months, and then only after having satisfactorily passed a bleeders test.

All riders returned to scale an average of 1kg overweight due to the weather conditions.
