RACE 1 (R011)

ZAYDAH, a course scratching as it was suffering from dehydration, will be required to pass a test (1000M/vet) before being entered to race again.

HISHAHITO which was up15kgs in body weight, HULK GENERAL which was up 17kgs in body weight and SOVEREIGN COMMAND which was up 21kgs in body weight since their last runs, were checked by the Club Veterinary Surgeon prior to the race and were passed fit to start.

This race was run in light rain.

Acting on veterinary advice that BIG KASH was behaving abnormally, the Stewards ordered the withdrawal of the gelding at 1:32pm. BIG KASH was impounded.

GOLDEN SAPPHIRE which was considerably slow to begin, will be required to pass a test (starting stall) before being entered to race again.

HES A CHARMER lugged out sharply on jumping, inconveniencing POPULAR and making contact with AMAKA.

HULK GENERAL was slow into stride.

At approximately the 400M, Jockey A Fiezan, the rider of POPULAR, dropped his whip. He was warned that a repetition of dropping the whip in future may lead to a penalty being imposed.

HES A CHARMER and AMAKA both hung out throughout.

All riders returned to scale an average of 1/2kg overweight due to the adverse weather conditions.

The Club Veterinary Surgeon reported that HES A CHARMER returned having cast its off hind plate.

RACE 2 (R012)

BIG DAWN, a course scratching as it was exhibiting signs of respiratory infection, will be required to pass a test (1000M/vet) before being entered to race again.

Trainer R Lines was fined RM100/- under MRA Rule 65(5)(b) for failing to produce the declared colours on PIRATE.

This race was run in light rain.

Acting on a report from the Starter that TEQUILA SUNRISE could not be boxed, the gelding was withdrawn by order of the Stewards at 2:06pm. TEQUILA SUNRISE will be required to requalify for an Official Race Trial before being entered to race again.

FLYING SWALLOW jumped awkwardly outwards.

EASTERN KING jumped awkwardly inwards, making contact with BEIJING CITY.

SHAKING HEAVEN jumped awkwardly inwards, inconveniencing MY DIAMOND and hung inwards in the straight.

RICH MANS WORLD was slow into stride.

At about the 350M, TRIPLE JIN shifted in badly from the whip. App CK Tan, the rider of TRIPLE JIN, in the presence of his Master, Trainer S Liew, was shown the video and warned that under similar circumstances in future he would be expected to put his whip down and make greater attempt to keep his horses on a straight course.

Jockey M Djulkifli, the rider of SMILES ALL ROUND, was shown the video of his ride in the latter stages. He was severely warned against the practice of not riding his horse out to the finish and that a repetition may lead to a penalty being imposed.

App J Low, the rider of PIRATE, reported that his horse did not act in the going.

All riders returned to scale an average of 1kg overweight due to the adverse weather conditions.

The Club Veterinary Surgeon reported that SHAKING HEAVEN returned having cast its off fore plate.

GENERAL REPORT(S)

APPEAL BY JOCKEY OH SENG HONG (SH OH) AGAINST HIS ONE (1) YEAR DISQUALIFICATION FOR CONTRAVENTION OF MRA RULE 44(8) IMPOSED BY THE STIPENDIARY STEWARDS IN CONNECTION WITH HIS RUNNING AND HANDLING OF RACEHORSE MIGHTY MIDGET IN RACE 10 (R055), 3RD DAY, SATURDAY, 12TH JANUARY 2008, PERAK TURF CLUB JANUARY MEETING 2008

The Perak Turf Club Racing Stewards heard the above appeal on 2nd January 2009.
After considering submissions from Jockey SH Oh and the Chief Stipendiary Steward, and after considering all the evidence, the Racing Stewards amended the charge to MRA Rule 44(10) for failing to ride to the satisfaction of the Stewards. Acting under MRA Rule 12(2)(z), the Racing Stewards suspended Jockey SH Oh for a period of 6 months with immediate effect and his deposit was refunded.

APPEAL BY SYCE CHOO KHAY SING AGAINST HIS DISQUALIFICATION OF THREE (3) YEARS FOR CONTRAVENTION OF MRA RULE 133(1)(m)(a)&(b) [AS PUNISHABLE UNDER MRA RULE 21(4)] IMPOSED BY THE STIPENDIARY STEWARDS IN CONNECTION WITH THE SEIZURE OF ITEMS FROM TRAINER NG KOK HOONGS (KH NG) STABLES AT THE PERAK TURF CLUB ON 18TH JUNE 2008

The Perak Turf Club Racing Stewards heard the above appeal on 2nd January 2009.
After considering submissions from Syce Choo Khay Sing and the Deputy Chief Stipendiary Steward, and after considering all the evidence, the Racing Stewards dismissed the appeal and the disqualification of three (3) years was confirmed to commence with effect from 18th June 2008 and to expire on 17th June 2011, both days inclusive. Syce Choo Khay Sings deposit was forfeited.


APPEAL BY TRAINER NG KOK HOONG (KH NG) AGAINST HIS FINE OF RM50,000/- FOR CONTRAVENTION OF MRA RULE 33(2) [AS PUNISHABLE UNDER MRA RULE 21(4)] IMPOSED BY THE STIPENDIARY STEWARDS IN CONNECTION WITH THE SEIZURE OF ITEMS FROM HIS STABLES AT THE PERAK TURF CLUB ON 18TH JUNE 2008

The Perak Turf Club Racing Stewards heard the above appeal on 2nd January 2009.
After considering submissions from Trainer KH Ng and the Deputy Chief Stipendiary Steward, and after considering all the evidence, the Racing Stewards dismissed the appeal. Trainer KH Ngs deposit was forfeited.


RACE 9 (R1465), 3RD DAY, SATURDAY, 27TH DECEMBER 2008, PENANG TURF CLUB DECEMBER MEETING 2008

The Stewards concluded an Inquiry into the running and handling of DARK GENERAL on 2nd January 2009. After taking evidence from Jockey A Cavallo, the rider of DARK GENERAL, and Trainer E Seet, and after reviewing the video, the Stewards informed Jockey A Cavallo that they were not entirely satisfied with his explanation, however, they decided to take no further action against him other than to warn him that in future he would be expected to ride all his horses in such a manner so as to leave no room for query. Furthermore, Jockey A Cavallo was also instructed that he would not be able to accept the ride on this horse again without first obtaining the Stewards prior permission.


INQUIRY CONCERNING URINE SAMPLE CODED J-2461 WHICH WAS TAKEN ON 14TH DECEMBER 2008 AT THE SELANGOR TURF CLUB DECEMBER MEETING 2008 AND RETURNED POSITIVE TO DEXTROMETHORPHAN & METABOLITE, WHICH ARE PROHIBITED SUBSTANCES IN TERMS OF MRA RULES

The Stewards concluded an Inquiry into the above matter on 2nd January 2009 at the Perak Turf Club. Evidence was taken from Jockey J Shankar, Prof Dr Tan Soo Choon, the Chief Analyst, Usains Holding Sdn Bhd, Dr Lam Yee Yoon and Dr B A Ramesh.

After considering all the evidence, Jockey J Shankar was found guilty to a charge under MRA Rule 136(3)(b) and the particulars of the charge were that a urine sample coded J-2461 submitted by him on 14th December 2008 at the Selangor Turf Club December Meeting 2008, on analysis, revealed the presence of Dextromethorphan & metabolite, which are prohibited substances under the MRA Rules.

Jockey J Shankar was suspended for a period of twelve (12) months with immediate effect from 2nd January 2009 and to expire on 1st January 2010, both days inclusive. He was advised of his Right of Appeal (6:05pm).

Jockey J Shankar subsequently exercised his Right of Appeal against the findings and penalty, and applied for a Stay of Sentence, which was granted.
